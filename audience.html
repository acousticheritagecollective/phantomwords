Phantom Words

I invite the audience to stroll through this universe of floating syllables and phantom words, to explore the sound space and allow themselves to enter into a trance. Each body's position reshapes the acoustic field and, with it, the words perceived.

Listen and let yourself fall into it.
Explore the sound space.
Allow yourself to enter into a trance.
What melodies do you hear?
What rhythms are formed?
What words do you hear?
Be part of the composition of this piece with your perception <3
